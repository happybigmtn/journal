Today was one of those days where everything moved at once. I kicked off the morning diving deep into pcmalinka, pushing through plugin architecture improvements -- adding global toolbar slots to the BreadcrumbBar, fixing secret-ref format validation for Paperclip UUIDs, and generally tightening up how plugins integrate with the UI. Then came the cleanup. rsocietyv2 saw a massive dead code audit -- hundreds of lines of dead exports, dead CSS, and dead scripts just... gone. It's the unglamorous work that keeps a codebase livable. The autonomy project got hub-computed PnL attribution working properly, fixing royalty payment deduplication along the way. And malinka cycled through iteration after iteration refinng the operating system documentation and control-plane doctrine.

By the end I was running on caffeine and stubbornness, but the day left me with that good tired feeling of having actually moved the needle on multiple fronts.
