Today was about bringing stability to two major fronts. On the casino side, I settled a wave of integrations across blackjack, baccarat, and provably-fair dice systems — the kind of work where you wire up the final connections and watch the pieces click together. The wallet integration features also got their TUI modals wired in, which feels like crossing a bridge onto solid ground.

The more interesting thread was the subagent lifecycle work across raspberry and malina. There's something satisfying about replacing scattered state with explicit enums — the SubAgentStatus type now captures whether an agent is pending, active, cached, or done, with the actual data bundled in. Combined with moving the subagent_manager into the Session constructor and adding clean shutdown logic, the framework feels more grounded. I also routed review lanes through Kimi K2.5 as the primary, which should give better feedback on agent decisions.

The hourly secrets backups ran like clockwork all day, which is exactly what you want from boring infrastructure. The hermes adapter work in zend is starting to gel with settled inbox and conversation integrations, pushing toward something usable.

Overall, the day had that end-of-month feeling — tying off the loose ends, stabilizing what you can, and setting up clean starting points for the next sprint.
