Today felt like a proper shipping day — bitino crossed the finish line with GATE-P3 fully verified and the live bet matrix hitting 35/35 games on the funded main chain. The fairness ordering verification from FAIR-001 also wrapped up, which has been a long time coming. There's something satisfying about seeing validation gates turn green after months of work.

Meanwhile bitpoker pushed through a cascade of RPM gates — RPM-12 tournament utility, RPM-11 9/10-max pilot, RPM-10 benchmark matrix, all the way down through the lower-numbered gates. The multiplayer engine contract (RT-03A) got recorded too, which unblocks the deterministic replay work in robopokermulti. Some stubborn bug work in there too — ST-02G is a testnet blocker that kept things interesting.

The autonomy repo was quieter in terms of new features but still grinding through the OPS-001 saga. Round-height skew turned out to be the real root cause rather than what we initially thought. The cold-start fix works but steady-state still stalls — that's the current blocker. Fleet convergence got relaxed from 3 to 30 which should help with stability. Plus RPC-001 and RPC-002 got hardened against pubkey-rotation bypass and rate-limit gaps.

Autodev kept the lights on with quota selector fixes and better error handling for when PI backends terminate during bug phases. Cross-repo review support is now in place which should help with the multi-repo workflow. All told, a solid day of pushing things across the line.
